Centauri Carbon Downloader
Créé par ashemka

This application can use FFmpeg for local timelapse video generation.
FFmpeg is a separate project with its own licensing terms.
See: https://ffmpeg.org/legal.html

If ffmpeg.exe is embedded during build, it is extracted locally at runtime to:
%LOCALAPPDATA%\CentauriCarbonDownloader\tools\ffmpeg.exe
